Site Plan: Local Book Club

Site Name

Name: Local Book Club

Reason for Selection: The name is simple, direct, and clearly represents a community-focused group centered around reading and discussing books.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the website is to provide a central hub for members of the local book club. It will offer information about upcoming meetings, summaries of past discussions, and resources for book enthusiasts.

Scenarios

1. What is the next book club meeting about?

2. How can I join the book club?

Color Scheme

Primary Color: #2c3e50 (Dark Blue) - Used for headers and titles.

Secondary Color: #ecf0f1 (Light Gray) - Used for backgrounds and sections.

Typography

Font 1: Arial - Used for body text and general content.

Font 2: Georgia - Used for headers and titles to give a classic, bookish feel.
