Featured Books

The Great Gatsby

Author: F. Scott Fitzgerald

Description: A novel set in the Jazz Age that tells the story of the mysterious millionaire Jay Gatsby and his obsession with Daisy Buchanan.

To Kill a Mockingbird

Author: Harper Lee

Description: A novel about the serious issues of rape and racial inequality, seen through the eyes of young Scout Finch in the Deep South during the 1930s.

1984

Author: George Orwell

Description: A dystopian novel set in a totalitarian society ruled by Big Brother. It explores themes of surveillance, propaganda, and individuality.

Pride and Prejudice

Author: Jane Austen

Description: A classic novel that explores the issues of marriage, money, and love in the society of the landed gentry of early 19th-century England.
